##vagrant
>Development environments made easy.
>
>【让开发环境变得更加容易】
>
>Create and configure lightweight, reproducible, and portable development environments.
>
>配置一个轻量级，可以移植的开发环境

>[Windows用户下载地址](https://www.vagrantup.com/downloads.html)

>_>vagrant
